Nutrition Philosophy

Our body's internal chemistry can be changed for the better or worse with foods. The way you define "good" or "bad" is deeply personal and I believe that the personal difference is where much of the conflict comes from when debating which diet is "best". Pushing your personal goals on another people is innately wrong because one does not know where another person gains their happiness in life. I argue, however, that there are goals that are at least partially true for all people such as: being pain free and being free of disease. From there, one may be looking to be as lean as possible to gain a speed and agility edge; gain as much mass as possible for leverage lifting massive weights; or just be looking to live a life free of knee pain following an ACL tear in high school. It is the my life mission to give people the guidance to chase whatever goals they want to chase.

Body Composition
