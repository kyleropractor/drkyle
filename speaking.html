Consulting

Physical health, ergonomics and nutrition are lifestyle issues that can be confusing. Contrasting information appears to abound, which leaves many confused and this includes individuals, health care practitioners, employers, and various organizations. The purpose of Dr. Steineman's consulting is to help reduce the confusion and make implementing changes a stress-free activity, whether it be for the home, practice, workplace, and/or organization. The consulting fee with Dr. Steineman is $200 per hour. Additional fees will be involved if information review, research, program planning, and/or travel are required.
