Dr. Kyle Steineman graduated from Manchester College and later, National Univeristy of Health Sciences in 2015. He later completed a sports nutrition fellowship under the guidance of Dr. Tim Stark at Northwestern Heaelth Sciences University. During his education at National, he was fortunate to have the opportunity to take 2 nutrition classes from Dr. David Seaman. After sustaining a cervical spine injury, Dr. Kyle integrated anti-inflammatory nutrition into his sports performance training and noticed decreased pain in conjunction with increase performance and benefits to his body composition. This began his career path to to learn about the implications nutritional deficits have on performance and injury recovery. Kyle went on to fuse this interest into his love of sports and began treating athletes of all ages and abilities. He has been fortunate to provide care for athletes ranging from adolescent to professional sports. He has traveled all over the US and many countries teaching treatment of athletic and soft tissue injuries. He has his International Certified Chiropractic Sports Physician destinction as of 2016.
